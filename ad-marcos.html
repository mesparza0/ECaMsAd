ECaMs Billboard Ad

Ever been stressed out while walking through the AS Building? You should stop by to check out the ECaMs Billboard. Check out our kiosk and not only learn new information but if you'd like to submit your flyer or picture, simply scan the QR code to upload your own content.

All user-submitted images or documents must be reviewed and accepted by an ECaMs billboard admin.

Our purpose is to get students walking through the AS building more involved with what is going on across campus!
